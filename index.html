Andrew Watts

Andrew Watts is a versatile ESL educator with over a decade of experience teaching English to students of all ages worldwide. From guiding young learners in Japan to coaching business professionals and university students online, Andrew brings a wealth of knowledge to every lesson. He holds a degree in International Relations, a TEFL certification, and an Educator's Certificate from the State of Florida.

With expertise bridging early childhood education and adult instruction, Andrew is adept at teaching beginner to advanced learners of all ages. His teaching methods are adaptable and innovative, designing customized learning paths that help young students build a strong foundation while assisting adults in mastering complex nuance and expression.

Andrew's commitment extends beyond just grammar and vocabulary; he fosters a global perspective in his classroom. His background in International Relations allows him to bridge cultural gaps, making him an exceptional teacher for anyone looking to master International English for travel, work, or personal growth.
